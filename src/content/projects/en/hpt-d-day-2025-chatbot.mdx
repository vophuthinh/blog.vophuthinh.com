At HPT D-DAY 2025, attendees and organizers needed instant answers for schedule, sessions, booths, and partner information.

To solve this, I built a zero-hallucination RAG chatbot grounded only on official event data:

- Event data from Excel was chunked and indexed into a vector store.
- GPT-4o answered only with retrieved context; when data was missing, it explicitly said so.
- n8n workflows connected retrieval + generation, while Google Sheets enabled real-time content updates.

Results:

- Handled **850+ attendee questions** during the event.
- Maintained **zero hallucination** policy for critical event information.
- Significantly reduced workload for the onsite support team.
