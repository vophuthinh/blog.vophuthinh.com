H.I.H automated business intelligence operations that were previously manual and inconsistent across teams.

The platform pipeline:

- Collected news from press, government portals, industry reports, and conferences.
- Classified content by strategic domains (banking, government, manufacturing, cybersecurity, AI).
- Generated summaries with actionable recommendations.
- Distributed weekly and monthly insights through Microsoft Viva Engage.

Impact:

- Won **3rd Prize** in HPT's "AI in Business" competition.
- HotNews Online reached **8,500+ views**.
- Industry News reached **~4,000 views**.
- Reached over **70% of HPT employees** across channels.
