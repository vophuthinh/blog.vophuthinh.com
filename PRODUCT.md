# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Vietnamese and English-speaking developers, tech leads, and engineering managers seeking practical insights on AI Agents, MLOps, AI Governance, and enterprise automation. They arrive from search or social, scan for credibility, and decide within seconds whether to read further. Ambient context: focused reading on laptop or phone, often during commute or between tasks.

## Product Purpose

Personal brand platform for Võ Phú Thịnh, AI Engineer at HPT Vietnam. The site establishes technical authority through real-world implementation stories, showcases completed projects, and serves as a professional presence that differentiates from generic dev blogs. Success looks like: visitors immediately perceive expertise, stay to read at least one full post, and remember the site.

## Brand Personality

Confident, practical, sharp. Speaks from implementation experience, not theory. No hand-holding, no hedging. Shows the work, names the tradeoffs, moves on.

## Anti-references

- Medium / Dev.to: too plain, no personality, commodity reading experience
- Generic SaaS templates: identical hero sections, feature grids, testimonial carousels
- Over-engineered portfolios: gratuitous parallax, 3D, page transitions that slow down access to content

## Design Principles

1. **Show the work**: Every design choice should demonstrate competence, not just claim it
2. **Confident restraint**: Bold when it matters, quiet when it doesn't. Never loud everywhere
3. **Immediate clarity**: No ornamental layers between the reader and the content
4. **Earned attention**: Visual hierarchy that guides, never decorates for its own sake
5. **Distinct identity**: Someone should recognize this site on a second visit without reading the name

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. Ensure contrast ratios meet 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text and UI components. Support both dark and light themes with equal care.
